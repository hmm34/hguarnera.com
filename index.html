Dr. Heather M. Guarnera

Since 2020, I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science in the Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences at The College of Wooster in Wooster, Ohio. I studied computer science at The University of Akron. The advisor for my Master's Thesis was Dr. Michael Collard. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Kent State University in 2020 under the supervision of Dr. Feodor Dragan.
